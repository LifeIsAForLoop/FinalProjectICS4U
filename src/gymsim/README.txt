GYM SIM User guide
GymSim is a gym simulator game where you control a player character
and interact with various gym equipment to level up different muscle groups. 
Use the WASD keys to move your character and 
click mouse when near gym machines to gain experience points (XP) and level up different muscle groups.

Game Overview:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Main Menu: The game starts with a main menu where you can begin your journey.
Gym: The game takes place in a fixed-camera gym environment where you can interact with exercise machines to level up different muscle groups.
Character: Your character has stats for various muscle groups like Back, Biceps, Calves, Chest, Core, Endurance, Forearms, Legs, Shoulders, and Triceps.
Movement: Use the WASD keys to move your character around the gym.
Controls:
W: Move Up
A: Move Left
S: Move Down
D: Move Right
Mouse Click: Interact with gym machines.
Features:
Player Stats: Your character's muscle stats (Back, Biceps, etc.) can be leveled up by interacting with the machines in the gym.
Movement Boundaries: The player can move within the bounds of the gym (with the edges of the screen as boundaries).


Once you reach a certain lvl the game will end
